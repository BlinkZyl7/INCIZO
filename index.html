Intelligence

7 indicators.
One verdict.

INCIZO only signals when 3 or more align. No noise. No guessing.

01
RSI (14)
Primary momentum
Overbought above 70. Oversold below 30. Divergence signals reversal before price moves.
02
EMA 9 / EMA 21
Trend direction
Fast/slow crossover. 9 above 21 = bullish. 9 below 21 = bearish. Never trade against it.
03
VWAP
Institutional benchmark
Where institutions buy and sell. Price above = bullish. Below = bearish. Most reliable in session open.
04
Support & Resistance
Key price levels
Horizontal levels where price reverses. Round numbers are the strongest on BTC. Trade the reaction.
05
Volume
Confirmation
High volume confirms moves. Low volume breakouts are almost always fake. Never enter without confirmation.
06
Candle Patterns
Entry timing
Engulfing, pin bar, doji, inside bar. On 5M only engulfing and pin bars are reliable. Everything else is noise.
07
Market Structure
Higher TF context
Higher highs and lows = uptrend. Lower highs and lows = downtrend. Always check 1H before any 5M trade.
